AI Smart Assign

An AI-powered enhancement to work reassignment — surfacing the best-fit people to take on a piece of work, ranked by role, skills and availability. Built at the company hackathon and shipped to production.

01

Finding the right person to take on the work

When reassigning work from one person to another, users had to manually scan the entire list of people — checking each individual's role, skills and availability by hand to judge who could realistically take it on. The process was slow, easy to get wrong, and got harder as teams grew.

What Changed

AI Smart Assign reads the work being transferred and the person it's moving from, then evaluates the wider team against three signals — role, skills and current availability. Rather than leaving users to search, it pre-ranks the people best suited to take on the work and surfaces them first.

02

From suggestion to assignment in one step

Even once a suitable person was identified, assigning still meant stepping back out into the full list to make the change — adding friction to what should be a quick decision.

What Changed

The top AI matches are presented in a dedicated suggestion banner, with the full list of people still available below for full control. Users can assign the work directly from the AI suggestion without leaving the flow — turning a manual search into a single confident action.
