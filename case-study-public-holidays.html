Public holidays

Solving availability to restore planning confidence

Quick read

Project overview

Planners couldn't see when staff were truly available across public holidays, sick leave and approved time off. Schedules were drafted on incomplete data, then corrected at the last minute — eroding trust between operations and the people on the ground.

I led the redesign of the availability surface inside the planning product, bringing public holiday logic, individual leave and team-level coverage into a single calendar view. The work shipped to ten enterprise customers and reduced last-minute schedule edits by a meaningful margin in the first quarter post-launch.

Responsibilities

Lead designer, research, prototyping

Target users

Workforce planners, ops managers

Adoption

10 enterprise customers in Q1

Outcome

Reduced last-minute schedule edits
